What is a crypto wallet?

A wallet holds your keys, not coins. Hot wallet: connected to the internet — convenient but has been hacked before. Cold wallet: a physical device kept offline — much safer for larger amounts, costs $50–$150.

Step 5 of 6 — What you'll need

Gather these before signing up

Every regulated exchange requires identity verification. Have these ready — you'll enter them directly on the platform's official website, not here.

Required by all platforms

Government-issued photo IDDriver's license or passport
Social Security NumberFor US tax compliance
Selfie / live photoFor identity verification
Email & phone numberFor 2-factor authentication
Bank account or debit cardTo fund your account

Security reminder: Always type the platform URL directly into your browser. Never click links from emails or ads. Verify the address carefully before entering any personal information.
